Hi I am Michael (Mike) Phan

Well you may see that my name is different now, I've changed it, as I have been through some rough earthy rocky roads. Going on the many different paths possible within about a painful and tragic year, I've been told all my life that I was glowing, flying, soaring that I could make the sun shine, no matter how dark the world around me was, I can make anyone feel right at home.

I started to follow a shadow one that I knowingly led me to a much darker, colder path, one with strangers, not friends or even family, one of many challenges was to try to hate the world because I loved it so much. I admit that I had everything I could ever want, but not exactly everything-everything I wanted to know the pain others had felt when I wasn't around,

I wanted to know the worst in world around me, I wanted to understand why there is evil in the world and why there isn't world peace! if we all do one good thing everyday wouldn't we slowly making the world as we know a much better place.
